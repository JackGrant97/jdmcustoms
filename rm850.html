  • arrow_drop_downProduct Review

    Product Review

    Advantages
    • Fully Modular So space can be saved
    • Fanless mode means quieter pc
    • Can power any single GPU/AIO liquid cooling build
    • can fit in and mid - full tower case

    Disadvantages
    • Expensive compared to other power supplies
    • Can be difficult to install due to variety of cables
    • Cables are rather ugly
    • Some cables are too short for larger cases


    Conclusion
    Corsairs RM 850 is a perfect power supply for smaller cases which lack room, this is due to its modular design which allows the user to remove cables not being used such as PCIE, Molex, and SATA cables. However, the cables supplied are ugly and don’t bend well so it may be difficult when needing to bend them in tight areas. However, it is possible to purchase aftermarket cables which are fully sleeved and with a colour of your choice. The RM850 can also power any single GPU and AIO liquid cooling system on the market with ease. However, if you are interested in SLI or crossfire then I would recommend a larger size such as 1000watt’s. The fanless mode reduces overall noise of the system theory allowing a quieter experience which ideal for content creators and gamers.

    Final Score

    5/10
