    Product Review

    Advantages
    • Provides a lot of room to cable manage
    • Able to install custom water cooling
    • Dust filters are extremely effective
    • Increased size allows for more airflow

    Disadvantages
    • Heavy
    • Expensive
    • Brushed aluminum surface easily becomes dirty
    • Some cables may be too short to use


    Conclusion
    Be quiet’s dark base pro 900 is a PC enthusiasts dream case. This is due to the overwhelming number of features provided in this case, for example the entire case including the aluminium side panel is covered in sound dampening foam therefore reducing the overall noise the system creates. The case also includes a wireless charging pad located on top of the case. And my favourite feature, everything inside the case can be taken out, and/or switch to the other side (this includes the motherboard tray and side panels which fit ether side). This allows for sections to be taken out and replaces with a custom water cooling. However, this case is extremely expensive and heavy. This case comes with legs but no wheels which means moving such a heavy case without damaging anything can prove to be difficult. I would highly recommend this case to anyone who can afford it.

    Final Score

    10/10
